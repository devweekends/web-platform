Fundamental Difference

Purpose ≠ Goals

Purpose (المقصد)
  • Who you want to be
  • Why you do what you do
  • Never ends; larger than lifetime
  • Example: serve humanity and uphold justice
Goals (الأهداف)
  • What you want to achieve
  • Specific and measurable
  • Has an end point
  • Example: build a home for parents; earn PKR 10 lakh/month

Purpose defines who you are. Goals define what you do.
